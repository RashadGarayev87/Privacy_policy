CarE – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [write today’s date]

1. Information Collection

CarE does not collect any personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

The app only reads vehicle sensor data (e.g., speed, RPM, fuel usage) directly from the car’s OBD-II system.

2. Use of Information

All collected data is processed and displayed locally on the user’s device.

No information is sent to external servers or shared with third parties.

3. Sharing of Personal Information

Since no personal information is collected, nothing is shared with anyone.

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is safe for all age groups, including children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on the official app page.
